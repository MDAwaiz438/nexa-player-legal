Privacy Policy – Nexa Player

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

1. Overview

Nexa Player is an offline media player designed to play audio and video files stored locally on your device. The App does not require user registration and does not provide online streaming services.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address, etc.)
  • Contacts or personal files
  • Usage analytics or tracking data

3. Local Data Processing

All media processing, including video playback, thumbnail generation, and subtitle handling, is performed entirely on your device.

We do not upload, access, or transmit your media files to any external server.

4. Permissions

The App may request access to device storage solely for the following purposes:

  • To read and display media files
  • To enable playback functionality

This access is used only locally and is never used to collect or transmit user data.

5. Third-Party Libraries

Nexa Player may use trusted open-source libraries and Android system components to enable media playback functionality. These components operate locally on your device and do not collect or transmit personal data.

6. Data Security

Since Nexa Player does not collect or transmit personal data, all information remains under your control on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Nexa Player does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.

8. Third-Party Services

The App does not include advertisements, analytics, or external tracking services.

If future updates introduce such features, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date above.

10. User Responsibility

You are responsible for the media content stored on your device. Nexa Player does not monitor or verify the legality of any files accessed through the App.
